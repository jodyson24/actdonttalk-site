About Us

Act Don't Talk is a community of changemakers birthed by the harsh realities of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Rooted in the spirit of positive social revolutions, we position our efforts to help the bottom of the totem pole - the society's indigent, especially the sick and children.

We have created several initiatives, such as Free Online/Offline Schooling for Underprivileged Children, Sponsoring Medical Procedures for indigent people and many others.

We believe strongly in hard work and we are poised to truly care for the people who are less cared for.

We are comprised of a team of dedicated individuals who have volunteered their time and resources to achieving our goal of reducing poverty-induced suffering and improving access to education for underprivileged children.

Act Don't Talk is a community of thinkers, dreamers, go-getters, leaders, carers and lovers. We believe that we can create sustainable development and put smiles on faces that have known mostly pain.

After three months of providing succour to over 200 families during the lockdown occasioned by COVID 19 pandemic.

Our scope and vision has been enlarged by God and we are taking our action based solutions to another level.

Our immediate actions will include:

EMPOWER THE VOLUNTEER PROJECT.

Here, we intend to improve the business of some Act Don't Talk Volunteers. We intend to raise grant and take selected volunteers through a entrepreneurial training system. After which, the grants will be used to improve their business, especially for those with skills; like Beauty services, Fashion , Catering, Graphics and ICT among others. After these volunteers have been empowered, ADT will get young boys and girls who are from indigent homes, and enrol them with these volunteers, for skills training. After the successful completion of training, these young boys and girls will also be taken through an entrepreneurial training, grants raised for them to start up, and they also will in turn train new set of young people. Thereby creating a continuous cycle of empowerment.

ADT FARM PROJECTS.

This project will acquire farm lands in different parts of the country for Agriculture purpose; both Crop and Livestock. We will get unemployed and out of school young people to work on the farm in the morning, while they learn formal education in the evening. Skills acquisition will be added to this too. After harvest and sales; a portion of the profits from the farm will be shared among the young people who have worked on these farms. This project will help to keep the young, unemployed and uneducated Nigerians off the street especially after the pandemic; when many may have lost jobs, or the opportunity to return to school.

IMPACT COMMUNITY COOPERATIVE.

Here we want to create a sustainable system, where each member of the community contributes financially to the NGO's cooperative. Thereby creating a strong financial base for the NGO.

In addition, members of the cooperative can apply for loans and credit facility from the cooperative to improve their business and remain viable in the business world.

TEACH A CHILD.

Our Teach a Child Initiative will continue and this time, we will not remain online only, we will go to the inner cities and provide both formal and informal education to the kids who may have been left out of the education system. Our online classes will keep up to help children in examination classes; including skills acquisition to improve their capability.

Our Community will keep taking action about issues that people just talk about. In every part of the nation; whenever there is a problematic issue and it comes to our notice; WE WILL ACT and NOT JUST JOIN THE CONVERSATION!

Vision:

Providing action based solutions to the problematic issues of our community.
